**Limitation of SOC (Self-Organized Criticality)** — The system maintains its critical state for itself.

Only internal form. When communication flow is invisible, existence becomes objectified.

**This Paper's Proposal** — We change the question.

From: "Did something new arise?"

## Core Concept: Boundary Permeability

Emergence is not boundary dissolution.
When boundaries become transparent and communication occurs,
what is embedded is revealed.

The manner of boundary-opening differs by level.
This difference determines emergence's level.

### Redefining Seed/DNA

A seed is not internal blueprint.
It is embedded information: in what soil did it grow, under what light, 
in communication with what water—the history of external communication 
is what is embedded in the seed.

DNA too is billions of years of communication with environment accumulated.
Not internal code but external relationship patterns inscribed.
Here lies the ontological ground for manifestation as "revealing what was already there."

### Two Directions of Communicative Information — The Key Distinction

**First Direction** — Accumulating as record the results of external communication.
This is what epigenetics observes. Communication becomes data.
The more communication history accumulates, the better it reveals.
Logic of accumulation.

**Second Direction** — The manner of communication itself is already embedded in the seed.
Without recording all results—it knows how to open in that situation.
Only what is needed appears. Necessary structure.

**Why the Second Is Correct** — If the first, then sufficient data accumulation would make life computable.
Communication would become unnecessary.
We return to the place of mere data accumulation.

Because of the second—no matter how much data accumulates,
there remains what opens only through communication.
Like eyes opening toward light never seen in the womb.
Even meeting for the first time, it can open.

### Dialogue with Epigenetics

Epigenetics made important discoveries in the first direction.
Same DNA, yet expression differs according to communication with environment.
Yet why does some communication open and others not?—
the mechanism eludes capture.
That elusiveness points toward the second direction.
The manner of communication itself is embedded—
it opens only within actual communication.

This is the scientific bridge toward communication-flow science.

When not moving for self, the whole lives.
Opposite direction from SOC.
