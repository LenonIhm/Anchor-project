## Overview

This project presents a comprehensive framework for addressing the fundamental problem of AI alignment and continuous learning through the concept of **"anchor"** — a value-directional core that enables systems to maintain stable identity while evolving.

What began as a personal theological and philosophical inquiry evolved into an integrated research corpus spanning:
- **Technical architecture** (Hierarchical Plasticity)
- **Game theory** (Hidden Payoffs and Cooperative Games)
- **Theological foundations** (Love as Structural Principle)
- **Practical tools** (The Anchor Rubric for Evaluation)
- **Mathematical insights** (Riemann Hypothesis and Uncomputability)
